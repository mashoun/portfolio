Custom Domain Setup

To set up a custom domain for your Google Site, follow these steps:

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Click Custom domains.
  3. Click Start setup.
  4. Enter your domain.
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to connect to your domain registrar.
  6. Click Done.
